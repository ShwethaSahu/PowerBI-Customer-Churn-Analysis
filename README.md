Welcome to my project

📊 Customer Retention & Churn Intelligence Dashboard:-
I built this interactive Power BI dashboard to tackle a really common business problem: understanding why subscribers leave a digital streaming service and finding ways to make them stay. 
Instead of just looking at raw numbers, this dashboard connects technical performance, plan types, and user demographics to tell the full story behind customer churn.


Takeaways :-
The Core Problem: The platform is hitting a **30.70% churn rate. That's a massive red flag and shows that a lot of users are dropping off instead of renewing.
The Tech Factor (Buffering Hurts): Interestingly, a major spike in churn (over 31%) happens when users experience buffering times between 1 to 4 seconds. It looks like minor, annoying delays frustrate core users enough to make them quit.
Free vs. Premium: Free plan users are the most volatile (+0.05 increase in churn), while Premium members are highly loyal and actually saw a decrease in churn (-0.06). 
Where it's Happening: Web and iOS users are leading the exit rates, sitting at roughly 31% each. 


Tools I Used:-
Power BI Desktop: For transforming raw data, building the underlying data model, and designing the final visual interface.
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): Used to write custom formulas for key metrics like Churn Rate, Active Subscribers, and MoM churn percentage changes.
PDF Export: I've uploaded a high-res PDF version right here in the repository so you can view the design layout perfectly without needing to download the raw files.
  

 My Recommendations for the Business:-
1. Fix the 1-4 Second Buffer: The engineering team needs to optimize video/audio delivery specifically to eliminate these brief lag times, as they are directly costing us customers.
2. Engage the Free Tier:Since Free users are leaving at high rates, we should trigger automated email campaigns offering them short, targeted Premium trials or exclusive content to show them the value of upgrading.
